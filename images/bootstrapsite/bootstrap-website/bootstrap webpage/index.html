WE SELL YOGURT!

Greek yogurt is a type of yogurt that is strained to remove the whey, resulting in a thicker, creamier yogurt with a higher protein content than regular yogurt.

Greek yogurt can be used in a variety of ways, such as a substitute for sour cream in recipes, as a base for dips and sauces, or as a healthy snack topped with fresh fruit or granola.
